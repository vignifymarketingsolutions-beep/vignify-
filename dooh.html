Digital Advertising FAQs

How long is a typical ad slot on an LED board?
Standard slots are 10 to 15 seconds in duration, repeating in a loop every 2-3 minutes. This guarantees your brand is seen hundreds of times per day.
Can I change my video content during the campaign?
Absolutely. Since it is digital, we can update your creative remotely within 24 hours. This is perfect for limited-time offers or countdowns.
Do you support audio in Cinema Ads?
Yes! On-Screen Cinema Ads include full Dolby surround sound. However, outdoor LED boards and Mall Lobby screens typically run without audio (visual only) to avoid noise pollution.
