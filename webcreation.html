Web Design FAQs

Common questions about building a website.

How much does a website cost?
The cost depends on the complexity (e.g., number of pages, functionality like e-commerce, custom features). We offer affordable packages for small businesses in Vellore and custom quotes for larger enterprises. Contact us for a free estimate.
How long does it take to build a website?
A basic corporate website typically takes 2-4 weeks. Larger E-commerce projects may take 4-8 weeks depending on product count and customizations. We provide a detailed timeline before starting.
Will my website work on mobile phones?
Absolutely. All our websites are designed with a Mobile-First approach. They are fully responsive and adapt perfectly to desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
Do I own the website after it's built?
Yes, 100%. Once the final payment is made, you have full ownership of the website code, design, and content. We do not hold your assets hostage.
