Have Questions?

Email Marketing FAQs

Common questions about automation and campaigns.

What email platform do you use?
We are platform agnostic but specialize in Klaviyo for E-commerce and Mailchimp/HubSpot for B2B. If you are already using a platform, we can optimize it. If not, we will recommend the best fit for your needs.
How do you prevent emails from going to spam?
We follow strict protocols: authenticating your domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), cleaning your list of inactive users, warming up IP addresses, and writing content that avoids spam trigger words.
Can you help with Cold Email Outreach?
Yes. We specialize in B2B cold outreach. We build targeted lead lists, write personalized sequences, and manage the sending process to generate qualified appointments for your sales team.
What is a Drip Campaign?
A drip campaign is a series of automated emails sent to people who take a specific action (like signing up or buying a product). We design these to nurture leads over time, educating them until they are ready to buy again.
