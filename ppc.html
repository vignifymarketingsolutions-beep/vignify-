Have Questions?

PPC Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Paid Advertising.

How much budget do I need for Google Ads?
There is no fixed minimum, but we recommend a starting budget that allows for enough data collection. For local businesses in Vellore, we can start with smaller budgets and scale up as we prove the ROI (Return on Investment).
Google Ads vs. Facebook Ads - Which is better?
Google Ads captures "high intent" (people actively searching for a solution). Facebook/Instagram Ads build awareness and target people based on interests. Most successful campaigns use a hybrid of both.
How fast will I see results?
Instantly. Once your campaign is approved and launched, your ads can start appearing on the first page of Google within hours, bringing immediate traffic to your site.
Do I pay Vignify or Google?
You pay Google directly for the ad spend (clicks), and you pay Vignify a management fee to optimize and run the campaigns. This ensures total transparency in your billing.
