Influencer Marketing FAQs

Everything you need to know about working with creators in Vellore.

How much does influencer marketing cost?
Costs vary wildly based on follower count and engagement. Micro-influencers in Vellore might charge in products or smaller fees (‚Çπ2k-10k), while larger influencers charge significantly more. We help you allocate your budget to get the maximum ROI.
Do you work with Micro-Influencers in Vellore?
Yes! We specialize in identifying local micro-influencers (5k-50k followers) who have highly engaged audiences in Vellore and surrounding Tamil Nadu districts. These creators often offer the highest ROI for local businesses.
How do you measure success?
We move beyond "likes." We track Reach, Engagement Rate, Click-Through Rate (CTR), and most importantly, Conversions using custom discount codes and tracked links to see exactly how much revenue the campaign generated.
Can we reuse the influencer's content?
Generally, yes, if negotiated correctly. We ensure that our contracts include usage rights, allowing you to use the high-quality User Generated Content (UGC) in your ads, website, and social media channels.
