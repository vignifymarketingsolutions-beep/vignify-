Have Questions?

Mobile Marketing FAQs

Common questions about SMS, WhatsApp and App Marketing.

Is Bulk SMS legal in India?
Yes, provided you register with the DLT (Distributed Ledger Technology) platform as per TRAI regulations. Vignify assists you with the entire DLT registration process to ensure your messages are approved and delivered legally.
Can I send promotional messages on WhatsApp?
Yes, but only via the WhatsApp Business API and using pre-approved templates. You cannot spam users. We help you get API access and design templates that follow WhatsApp's strict commerce policies to avoid number blocking.
How does Geofencing work in Vellore?
We create a virtual fence around a specific location (like your store or a competitor's). When a user with a smartphone enters this area, we can trigger ads to appear on their apps or browsers, driving foot traffic to your business.
What is ASO?
ASO stands for App Store Optimization. It's like SEO for apps. We optimize your app's title, description, screenshots, and reviews so it ranks higher in Play Store and App Store searches, leading to more organic downloads.
