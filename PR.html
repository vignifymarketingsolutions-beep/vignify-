Media FAQs

How much does a Newspaper Ad cost in Vellore?
Costs vary by publication (e.g., Daily Thanthi vs The Hindu) and size (Classified vs Full Page). However, as an agency, we secure rates that are typically 15-20% lower than standard card rates for our clients.
Can you guarantee Media Coverage?
For Paid Advertising (Ads/Commercials), yes, coverage is guaranteed. For PR (News Articles), it depends on the newsworthiness, but our strong relationships with editors ensure your press releases get high priority consideration.
What is the best time for Radio Ads?
The "Prime Time" for radio is usually Morning (8 AM - 11 AM) and Evening (5 PM - 8 PM) when commuting is at its peak. We help you target these specific time bands.
