Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about growing your brand on social media in Vellore.

Which platforms should my business be on?
It depends on your audience! For B2B companies in Vellore, we focus on LinkedIn to reach decision-makers. For lifestyle, food, fashion, or retail, we prioritize Instagram and Facebook visuals. We analyze your business model to choose the platforms where your actual customers hang out.
What is the difference between Organic and Paid SMM?
Organic SMM is free posting to build long-term relationships, brand loyalty, and community. Paid SMM (Facebook/Instagram Ads) involves paying the platform to show your content to new people immediately based on demographics. We recommend a hybrid strategy for the best results.
Do I need a big budget for Facebook Ads?
Not at all. You can start with a small daily budget to test the market. Our job at Vignify is to optimize your ads (A/B testing) so you get the lowest Cost Per Click (CPC) and highest return, regardless of your starting budget.
Will social media actually generate sales/leads?
Yes. While "likes" are nice, our primary focus is conversion. We use strategies like Lead Gen Forms, "Shop Now" buttons, and retargeting campaigns to capture users who are ready to buy. We turn passive scrollers into paying clients.
Do I need to provide the photos and videos?
We can work with what you have, but we also offer full content creation services. Our team can visit your location in Vellore to shoot professional photos and Reels, or we can design high-quality graphics remotely using your brand assets.
How often will you post on my accounts?
Consistency is key to beating the algorithm. Depending on your package, we typically post 3 to 5 times per week, including a mix of static posts, carousels, and Reels. We also engage with your community daily via Stories.
How do you measure success?
We provide transparent monthly reports. We track metrics that matter to your bottom line: Website Clicks, Leads Generated, Reach, and Engagement Rate. You will always know exactly how your investment is performing.
My business is B2B. Does social media work for me?
Absolutely. For B2B clients, we shift the focus to LinkedIn Authority Building and educational content. We position your brand as an industry leader to attract partnerships and high-ticket clients, rather than just chasing "likes."
