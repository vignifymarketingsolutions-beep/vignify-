SEM Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about paid search.

What is the difference between SEO and SEM?
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of earning traffic organically (for free) over time. SEM (Search Engine Marketing) involves paying for traffic (PPC) to get instant results. Ideally, you should do both for maximum visibility.
How much does Google Ads cost?
You control the budget. You can start with as little as ‚Çπ500/day. However, the cost per click (CPC) depends on your industry and competition. We help you estimate a budget that will provide enough data to generate a positive ROI.
How soon will I see results?
Instantly! Once your campaign is set up and approved by Google (usually within 24 hours), your ads can start appearing on the first page of search results, driving immediate traffic to your site.
Do you offer Bing Ads management?
Yes. While Google is the giant, Bing (Microsoft Ads) often has less competition and cheaper clicks. For B2B clients and certain demographics, we highly recommend running campaigns on Bing alongside Google.
