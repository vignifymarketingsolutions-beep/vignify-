How long does it take to see SEO results?
SEO is a long-term investment. Typically, you will start seeing initial movement in rankings within 3 months. However, significant organic traffic growth and consistent lead generation usually occur between 6 to 12 months. We focus on sustainable, long-term growth rather than quick, risky fixes.
Do you provide Local SEO for Vellore businesses?
Absolutely. We specialize in Local SEO strategies. We optimize your Google Business Profile (formerly GMB), build local citations, and create location-specific content to help you rank for "Near Me" searches in Vellore, Katpadi, and surrounding Tamil Nadu regions.
How will I know if the SEO is working?
Transparency is our core value. We provide detailed monthly reports that show your keyword rankings, organic traffic growth, and conversion metrics. We use premium tools like Google Analytics, Search Console, and SEMrush to give you data you can trust.
Should I choose SEO or Google Ads (PPC)?
It depends on your goals. Google Ads provides immediate traffic but stops when you stop paying. SEO provides long-term, sustainable traffic that generates ROI for years. For most businesses in Vellore, we recommend a hybrid strategy to maximize results.
Do you do E-commerce SEO for online stores?
Yes! We have extensive experience optimizing online stores built on WooCommerce, Shopify, and Magento. We focus on product schema, category page optimization, and reducing cart abandonment to drive sales, not just clicks.
Can you guarantee a #1 ranking on Google?
No ethical agency can guarantee a #1 ranking because Google's algorithm changes constantly. However, Vignify guarantees White-Hat practices and a data-backed strategy that consistently improves your visibility and traffic.
