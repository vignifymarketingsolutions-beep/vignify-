Frequently Asked Questions

Can you customize Digital Marketing training for our team?
Absolutely. We conduct a pre-training audit to understand your team's skill level and customize the modules (e.g., LinkedIn for Sales, Social Media for HR) to meet your specific business objectives.
How do you help with CSR promotion?
We send a production crew to document your CSR activities (photos/videos). We then create press releases for newspapers, social media content, and impact reports to share with stakeholders and the public.
Do you handle confidential internal communications?
Yes. We sign strict NDAs with all our corporate clients. Whether it is a CEO's message, policy change communication, or internal crisis management, your data and strategy remain secure with us.
