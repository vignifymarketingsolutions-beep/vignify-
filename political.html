Political Management FAQs

How early should we start campaign management?
Ideally, 6-12 months before the election. Reputation building (ORM) takes time. Last-minute campaigns are about "noise," but long-term campaigns are about "trust."
Can you handle negative news online?
Yes. Our ORM team works to suppress negative links, remove fake reviews where legally possible, and flood the digital space with positive, verified achievements of the candidate.
Do you do printing for Rally Banners?
Absolutely. We have vendor partnerships for high-volume Flex Printing, Cloth Banners, and Cutouts in Vellore. We handle the design, printing, and even the installation logistics for large rallies.
