Content Marketing FAQs

Everything you need to know about our writing services.

How often should I publish a blog?
Consistency is key. For most businesses in Vellore, we recommend 2-4 high-quality articles per month. This frequency signals to Google that your site is active and allows you to cover a wide range of keywords.
Do you use AI to write content?
We use AI tools for research, outlining, and SEO analysis, but the actual writing is done by human experts. This ensures your content has a unique voice, emotional connection, and factual accuracy that AI alone cannot provide.
What is the difference between Copywriting and Content Writing?
Content Writing (Blogs, Articles) is designed to educate and inform over the long term. Copywriting (Ads, Landing Pages) is designed to persuade the reader to take an immediate action (Buy, Sign Up). We offer both.
