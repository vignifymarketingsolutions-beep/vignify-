CRO Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about optimizing your website.

What is Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)?
CRO is the systematic process of increasing the percentage of website visitors who take a desired action (like buying a product or filling out a form). It involves understanding how users move through your site and removing barriers that stop them from converting.
How is CRO different from SEO?
SEO focuses on driving traffic *to* your website. CRO focuses on what that traffic does *once they arrive*. SEO gets them to the door; CRO gets them to buy. You need both for maximum growth.
Do I need a lot of traffic to start CRO?
Ideally, yes. To run statistically significant A/B tests, you need a decent amount of traffic. However, for smaller sites in Vellore, we use heuristic analysis and user recording sessions (Hotjar) to make impactful improvements even with lower traffic.
How long does it take to see results?
Some changes (like fixing broken forms or slow loading speeds) yield immediate results. Complex A/B tests usually run for 2-4 weeks to gather enough data. We aim for continuous, incremental improvements month over month.
