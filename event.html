Event FAQs

How much notice do you need for a Product Launch?
For large-scale launches requiring custom fabrication and permissions, we recommend 3-4 weeks. However, for smaller activations or college setups, we can mobilize in 7-10 days.
Do you handle Political Campaign events?
Yes. We have experience managing logistics, stage setup, sound systems, and crowd control for large-scale political rallies and roadshows across Vellore and surrounding districts.
What is required for an AR/VR Installation?
We need a dedicated indoor or shaded outdoor space (approx 10x10ft) and a power supply. We provide the headsets, screens, software, and technical staff to guide users.
